Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Set your rules once. Browseway handles the rest — quietly, from your menu bar.

Source App Rules

Links from Slack open in Chrome. Links from Telegram open in Safari. Each app gets its own browser — automatically.

Per-App

URL Pattern Matching

Route github.com to one browser, google.com to another. Bare domains, wildcards, and substring matching.

Wildcards

URL Cleaning

Strips utm_*, fbclid, gclid and other tracking junk. Expands bit.ly and t.co short links before routing.

Privacy

Auto Updates

Built-in Sparkle updater checks for new versions automatically. Always stay current, zero effort.

Sparkle

Pause Rules

One click to pause all rules from the menu bar. Every link goes straight to your fallback browser until you resume.

Quick Toggle

Shortcuts Integration

Create rules, route URLs, and manage browsers through Apple Shortcuts. Automate your workflow.
